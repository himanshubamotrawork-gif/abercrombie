Frequently Asked Questions

What is this programme? +
This is a Product Reviewer programme where participants provide feedback on partner products and services. In return, you get a chance to win an Abercrombie & Fitch $500 Shopping Gift Card.
How do I qualify for the gift card? +
To qualify, you need to enter your basic details and complete 4–5 partner offers. Once all offers are completed and verified, you will be entered into the reward process for your chance to receive the gift card.
Is this affiliated with Abercrombie & Fitch? +
No. This programme is operated by a third-party rewards platform and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Abercrombie & Fitch. The gift card is offered as a reward incentive.
How many offers do I need to complete? +
You need to complete 4–5 partner offers to finish the review process. Each offer will be clearly outlined after you continue. Make sure to complete all required steps to be eligible for the reward.
Who is eligible to participate? +
This programme is available to residents of the UK, Australia, Canada, and the USA. A mobile device is required to participate, and only one participation per household is allowed.
